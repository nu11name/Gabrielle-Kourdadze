Arrêt sur images

This video made by Thais Despont presents my exhibition “Arrêt sur image” (December 2022 - January 2023), with an excerpt from the immersive sound installation that I created for the occasion, entitled “ Solitudes: composition à plusieurs voix ”. With the voices of : Cédric Welsch, Francis Leonesi, Morgane Goaziou, Romain Francisco, Manon Tissier, Nina Dotti, Inaya Trottemant, Gabrielle Kourdadzé, Jennyfer Trottemant, Armand Leroy, Baptiste Briouze, Thibaud Pislor, France Albert, Ecem Cicek, Hélène Deregnier, Fabienne Degouy, Lya Descamps, Tina Kourdadzé, Daniele Rezzi, Romain Daitré, Raoul Gogly, Agathe Vidal, Taor Lipietz-Tropp, Olivier Denizet, Simon Voirin, Françoise Krief, Agnès Kourdadzé, Lucie Troisbé-Baumann, Garance Chansigaud-Fargepallet, Emmanuel Guillon, Robin Summa.summa , Léa-Amélia Casagrande.
